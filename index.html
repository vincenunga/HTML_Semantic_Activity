About

Our Story

At Team Seas, our journey began with a shared vision of protecting our planet's most precious resource ‚Äì our oceans.
We believe that collectively, we can make a significant impact and preserve the beauty and diversity of our marine
ecosystems for generations to come.

Our story started when a group of passionate individuals from diverse backgrounds came together, united by a common
purpose: to combat the pressing issue of marine pollution. Witnessing the devastating effects of plastic waste and
pollution on our oceans, we knew we had to take action. Inspired by the success of our predecessors at Team Trees,
we set out to create a movement that would address the urgent need for cleaner, healthier seas.

Driven by this mission, we launched Team Seas, a global initiative that brings people, organizations, and influencers
together to remove trash from our oceans and coastlines. We set an ambitious goal to remove 30 million pounds
(about 13.6 million kilograms) of garbage from the ocean by the end of 2025. Our story is one of hope, determination,
and collaboration, as we work tirelessly to protect and restore the beauty of our marine world.
